Instructions

Adding a Student Ticket:
1. Go to the "Add Tickets - Student" page.
2. Enter the Student # and click "Verify Student".
3. Enter the Ticket # and submit.
Adding a Guest Ticket:
1. Go to the "Add Tickets - Guest" page.
2. Enter the First and Last name of the Guest.
3. Enter the Student # of the student that invited them.
4. Enter the Ticket # and submit.
Searching for Tickets:
1. Go to the "Search" page.
2. Enter either the Student # or Ticket #.
3. Submit to view the ticket details.
Editing Tickets:
1. Go to the "Edit Info" page.
2. Enter the Ticket # you wish to edit and click Search.
3. You may now edit any associated information.
4. Once you are finished, click Update Ticket.
